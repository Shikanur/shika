My chamomile voyage

or how I am falling in love with travelling, nature and learning
new things, trying to build my small happy world with
big dreams.

In the past I couldn't travel much due to various reasons:

financial problems,
study, part-time
work, or simply
lack of desire.

And I didn't want anything special.
but since last year I have been dying just
thinking about how beautiful our planet is and I
can’t wait to see all the beautiful places on it.
and here comes the reason why I want to change
my profession, starting to do something
completely foreign to me, but which brings me
so much pleasure and also gives me the
opportunity to travel in the future and at the
same time work remotely.
Below you can see an incomplete list of things
I’ve been trying to do and then places I visited,
am crazy about and want to visit no matter the

For a long time I lived without dreams. very long time. The loss of a loved one killed all my desire for life. but now I understand that we
live once and we must live in such a way that there are no regrets later. one must live, not just exist. and after I understood this,
I realized that I wanted to live; my soul became so calm. I really like the way I think now, how I love myself and life.
